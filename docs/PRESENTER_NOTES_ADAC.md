## Speaker tips (general)

- **Pacing:** target ~4 minutes per major section; 90 minutes total leaves ~10 minutes for buffer and discussion.
- **Pricing scenarios:** if ADAC opens with their own pricing proposal first, switch to Scenario A (Cmd/Ctrl+1) so prices read "To be agreed" — discuss philosophy first, numbers second.
- **If a slide doesn't apply:** press **→** through it rather than skipping silently; the audience doesn't see the page count.
- **If asked something not on the slide:** use **M** to open the sidebar, jump to the relevant section, present from there. The sidebar auto-closes after selection.
- **If the network is slow:** the bundled fallback already has every chart number; pages still render. Mention nothing about it.
- **If asked for the IBAN or bank details:** "We'll send those operationally with the contract draft" — do not read them from the room.
- **Press F** for fullscreen at the start. **Press F again** to exit if you need to switch to a different application.

Good luck.
