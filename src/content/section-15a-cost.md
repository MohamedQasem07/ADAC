Cost predictability is the headline operational benefit of the flat-rate framework. ADAC knows the cost of a case the moment the package is chosen — before any treatment begins.

- One number per clinical presentation, agreed pre-authorization
- No itemized consumables, no observation-time surcharges, no time-of-day premium
- Budget forecasting becomes mechanical for the Red Sea cluster
- Reserve setting per case is determined by the catalogue, not by the invoice
- Variability moves from invoice-level to case-mix level, which is easier to model
