eyebrow: "§13.1"
summary: "Each flat rate reflects the clinical work — not the hotel star rating or the time of day."

---

The catalogue is calibrated against the clinical work actually performed. The same package costs the same in a clinic, in a five-star hotel room, or in a budget hotel.

- Each package priced against the real clinical scope (exam, medication, observation, follow-up)
- Mode of delivery does not change the package price
- Time of day does not change the package price
- Pediatric and adult equivalents priced independently to reflect actual workload
- Scenario-based negotiation supported — see §13.2 for what is always included
