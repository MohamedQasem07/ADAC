When the AG Holder is too unwell to travel, the team comes to them. Hotel-room visits are the operational default, not the exception.

- Physician + nurse arrive with full portable equipment
- Same package price as a clinic visit
- Most clinical workups completable bedside (IV therapy, ECG, wound care, suturing, X-Ray)
- German-speaking physician available on request
- Privacy and comfort of the hotel room rather than a clinic waiting area
