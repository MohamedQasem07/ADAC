The clinical team is sized for outpatient surge rather than steady-state, with physicians, nurses, and case coordinators reachable in four languages. The Operations Department centralizes communication with assistance partners and insurers.

- Physicians on call across all 10 locations, with rotating night cover
- Nursing teams trained for IV therapy, wound care, and portable diagnostics
- Case coordinators in the Operations Department handle insurer communication, pre-authorization, and reporting
- Languages covered: English, German, Arabic, Russian
- German-speaking physician available on request for hotel-room visits
- Dedicated radiographer and lab capability at the larger network locations
