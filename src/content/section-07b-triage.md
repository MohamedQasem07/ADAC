Triage runs in parallel with administrative setup. A physician or trained triage nurse reviews the presenting complaint by phone or hotel arrival and classifies severity.

- Green: simple outpatient, package selected directly
- Yellow: outpatient with labs, imaging, or extended observation
- Red: escalation criteria present — assistance company notified, ER pathway opened
- Mode of service (clinic, hotel-room, mobile unit) chosen from clinical fit
- Pediatric and senior-specific protocols applied where relevant
