The receiving emergency facility receives a structured handoff before the AG Holder arrives, so triage at the door is not starting from zero.

- Pre-arrival call: presenting complaint, vitals, stabilization given
- Structured written handoff at arrival (SBAR-style)
- HMC physician remains the point of contact for clinical history
- HMC case coordinator remains the point of contact for ADAC
- Transition of clinical responsibility recorded explicitly
