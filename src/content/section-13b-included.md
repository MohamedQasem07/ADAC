## Context

Every package, regardless of category or severity tier, includes the same operational basics. These items are inside the flat rate by definition and are never added on top of the invoice later. The list exists so that ADAC and HMC share an explicit understanding of what the package number actually covers.

## Key points

- **Doctor examination.** Full clinical examination by the treating physician, including vital-signs assessment, is inside every package.
- **Standard medication.** Medications within the clinical scope of the package are included; there are no surprise pharmacy line items.
- **Medical report.** The structured same-day case summary is part of the package, not an add-on document.
- **Mode of delivery.** Clinic, hotel-room, or mobile delivery is included in the same flat rate.
- **Basic consumables and one follow-up.** Standard consumables and one control or follow-up visit, where clinically indicated, are inside the rate.

## What this means for ADAC

ADAC can authorize a package knowing exactly which clinical elements are bought with that number. There is no parallel list of small add-ons that surface only on the invoice, and the structural separation between "included" and "quoted separately" is published rather than discretionary.
