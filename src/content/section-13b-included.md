Every flat-rate package, regardless of category or scenario, includes the same operational basics. These are never added on top later.

- Doctor examination and vital-signs assessment
- Prescribed medications within the package scope
- Basic consumables (cannula, dressings, syringes, etc.)
- Detailed Medical Report (English; German on request)
- Package-coded invoice
- One control or follow-up visit where clinically indicated
