---
title: "Ambulance Activation"
eyebrow: "§11.2"
summary: "How HMC dispatches ambulance or semi-ambulance transport in escalation cases."
layout: "editorial"
---

When escalation is triggered, ambulance or semi-ambulance transport is dispatched from the nearest network-supported transport unit.

- Decision to escalate is made by the treating physician at the bedside
- Ambulance with paramedic capability dispatched for unstable patients
- Semi-ambulance transport for stable cases requiring imaging or hospital transfer
- Stabilization continues during transport
- Receiving facility informed in advance and prepared for arrival
