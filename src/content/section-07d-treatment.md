The physician arrives at the agreed location with the equipment matched to the package. Treatment proceeds against the published scope.

- Physician + nurse arrive with package-matched equipment
- Standard intake: vital signs, history, examination
- Treatment delivered per package scope (IV, medication, imaging, suturing, etc.)
- Real-time clinical notes captured for the medical report
- Any deviation from package scope flagged to the case coordinator immediately
