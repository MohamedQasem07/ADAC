The pilot runs across the high-volume German summer season — the right window to stress-test the framework with real case volume.

- Three-month operational pilot: July, August, September
- All eligible ADAC cases handled under the flat-rate framework
- Weekly active-case digest to both teams
- Monthly performance reports as defined in §9.3
- Mid-pilot review at end of August to flag any operational adjustments
- Pilot ends 30 September; data closed and prepared for the October review
