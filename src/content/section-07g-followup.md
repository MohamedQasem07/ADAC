---

The journey closes with a follow-up phone check and any indicated review visits. Cases close on agreed clinical and administrative criteria.

- 12-hour safety-net phone check for moderate-and-above outpatient cases
- Follow-up visits included in the package where indicated (suture removal, dressing change, re-assessment)
- Pediatric cases include a next-day follow-up call by default
- Case closed jointly between HMC Operations and ADAC
- Aggregate case data feeds the monthly performance report (§10.4)
