The northern and southern extremes of the Red Sea tourist corridor are covered by combining a fixed location with on-call transport and a mobile clinic deployment.

- Soma Bay Clinic — fixed location anchoring Safaga and the northern resort zone
- Marsa Alam covered by network extension: on-call physician, semi-ambulance transport, and remote-triage protocol
- Hotel-room and dive-resort visits available across the extended zone
- Cases requiring imaging or extended observation are routed to the nearest equipped location
- Escalation routes to ER are pre-defined for each zone
