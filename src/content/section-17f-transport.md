## Question

Are ambulance dispatch and inter-facility transfers quoted separately from the flat-rate package, and what is the prior-comms protocol for long-distance evacuations?

## HMC suggested position

- **Ambulance dispatch outside the flat-rate.** Documented as a separate event with full timestamps and routing detail — never absorbed into the package price.
- **ER coordination and admission outside the flat-rate.** Hospital admission and the coordination work around it are billed and documented as standalone events.
- **Mode of outpatient delivery is price-neutral.** Clinic, hotel-room, or mobile delivery for outpatient care does not change the package price.
- **Long-distance evacuation needs prior-comms.** Marsa Alam to Hurghada hospital corridor follows the documented protocol with ADAC notified before the transfer begins.

## What we need ADAC to confirm

Confirm the transport billing pathway and the prior-comms protocol — including the named ADAC contact who receives the long-distance evacuation notification.

## Risk / note

The Marsa Alam corridor evacuation can run 2 to 3 hours road time — the protocol must specify exactly who calls whom and at what point in the transfer.
