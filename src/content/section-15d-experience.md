When the cost is known up front, ADAC can give the AG Holder a clear, immediate answer. The AG Holder spends the case worrying about their health, not about the bill.

- Verbal confirmation of coverage at the bedside
- Same Detailed Medical Report standard for every case
- German-speaking physician available on request for hotel-room visits
- 12-hour safety-net follow-up call standard for moderate cases
- AG Holder feedback collected anonymously and summarized monthly
