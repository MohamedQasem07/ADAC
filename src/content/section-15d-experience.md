## Context

The traveler experience determines how a case is remembered long after the medical issue resolves. When cost, language, and documentation are handled cleanly at the point of care, the AG Holder spends the case focused on their health rather than chasing clarifications between ADAC and a foreign clinic.

## Key points

- **No surprise bills at reception.** Coverage is confirmed at the bedside against the agreed package, so the traveler is not asked to sign open-ended financial forms.
- **One discharge document.** Every case closes with the same Detailed Medical Report format, in English, suitable for the AG Holder's family physician at home.
- **Language handled at point of care.** Reception, clinical conversation, and discharge work in English by default, with German support where staffing allows.
- **Predictable handover to ADAC.** The traveler receives one clear explanation of what happens next administratively — there is no second phone call required from the hotel room.
- **Feedback collected anonymously.** Post-case feedback is summarized and reported back to ADAC monthly, not curated for marketing.

## What this means for ADAC

The AG Holder reaches home with a complete file and no open questions. Case-related complaints back to ADAC drop, and the partnership earns the kind of trust that only repeated, undramatic execution produces.
