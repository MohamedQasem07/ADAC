June is the operational preparation month. Both teams get the channels, templates, and triage protocols ready before any AG Holder is touched by the new flow.

- HMC Operations briefed on the agreed package list and exclusion criteria
- ADAC case coordinators briefed on the catalogue and the pre-authorization flow
- Communication channels established (email, hotline, optional portal)
- Reporting templates approved by both sides
- Dry-run cases simulated end-to-end before the pilot start
- Joint go/no-go review at end of June
