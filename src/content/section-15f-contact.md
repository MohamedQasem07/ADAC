ADAC interacts with one Operations Department and, within each case, one named coordinator. No re-routing between multiple regional clinics.

- One Operations Department for the partnership, with named senior coordinator
- One case coordinator per case for its full lifecycle
- One reporting cadence across all 10 locations
- One escalation hotline; one written reporting channel
- Monthly performance review with the same operational counterpart
