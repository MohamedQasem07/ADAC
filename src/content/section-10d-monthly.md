The monthly performance report consolidates the metrics defined in §9.4 into a single document that supports ADAC's operational review.

- Aggregate case volume by month, by category, and by location
- Package mix — which catalogue codes were used and how often
- SLA hit-rate against acknowledgement, triage, and dispatch targets
- Escalation rate and breakdown of escalation reasons
- Operational notes: any unusual patterns, outbreaks, or process changes
