## Question

Do we confirm the 1 July to 30 September 2026 pilot window with the October joint review as the decision point?

## HMC suggested position

- **Pilot runs 1 July to 30 September 2026.** The October review is held on the first complete month of stable operational data.
- **Volume target ~30 cases minimum.** Needed to make the data meaningful for the review, with corridor-wide coverage from day one across all four zones.
- **June used for preparation.** Contact naming, channel setup, first-case checklist, and format sign-off all locked before 1 July go-live.
- **October decides extension and direction.** Joint review covers extension, scope changes, and the direction for the formal partnership contract.

## What we need ADAC to confirm

Confirm the pilot dates and the October review date, and nominate the ADAC contacts who will lead the June preparation phase from the assistance side.

## Risk / note

If pilot volume runs below 30 cases by end of September, the review extends one month to give the data enough stability for a clean decision.
