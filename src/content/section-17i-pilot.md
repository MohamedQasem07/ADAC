Define the perimeter and the success criteria of the July–September pilot so the October review can be honest.

- Zones in scope (default: all 4 — Hurghada, Sahl Hasheesh, Safaga, Marsa Alam)
- AG-Holder eligibility (default: all ADAC AG Holders presenting in scope zones)
- Modes of service in scope (default: all three — clinic, hotel-room, mobile)
- Success criteria: SLA hit-rates, dispute count, case-mix coverage, AG-Holder feedback
- Mid-pilot review checkpoint (end of August)
- Decision framework for the October joint review
