The mobile clinic unit is parked at the hotel gate or in a resort common area. Used when multiple travelers may need attention, when the case needs extended observation, or when the hotel cannot accommodate room-based treatment.

- Same package price as a clinic visit
- Full IV therapy station, observation seating, ECG, portable X-Ray
- Useful for resort outbreaks (food poisoning, allergic clusters) where multiple AG Holders may need care
- Parked at hotel gates with resort-management coordination
- Recommended for: extended observation (3+ hours), simultaneous multi-patient triage, on-resort outbreak response
