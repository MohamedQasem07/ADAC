---
title: "The Cost Predictability Problem"
eyebrow: "§4.2"
summary: "Itemized billing produces unpredictable totals, disputed line items, and slow case closure."
layout: "editorial"
---

Itemized invoicing turns every case into a negotiation. Each line is judged separately, queries multiply, and a routine outpatient case can stay open for weeks while a single consumable is re-priced.

- Item-by-item invoices generate disputes on consumables, observation time, and "extras"
- Pre-authorization decisions are slowed by uncertainty about final cost
- Cases stay open longer; assistance teams carry a larger active load
- The traveler hears unclear answers about what is and is not covered
- Reconciliation work after the case is operationally expensive on both sides
