## Context

The same-day case summary is the clinical record that travels with the invoice. It is captured at the point of care, signed by the treating physician, and completed on the same day the case is closed. This timing keeps the clinical detail accurate while it is still fresh and gives ADAC a usable document inside the operational window.

## Key points

- **Diagnosis with ICD-10.** Primary and secondary diagnoses are coded so the record integrates cleanly with ADAC's existing case files.
- **Procedures performed.** Every procedure carried out during the case is listed against the package scope.
- **Medications given.** Drug name, dose, route, and time of administration are recorded for each medication.
- **Physician sign-off.** The treating physician signs the document, which establishes clinical accountability.
- **Same-day completion target.** The summary is closed and dispatched on the day the case itself closes.

## What this means for ADAC

Each closed case arrives as a self-contained clinical record rather than a placeholder awaiting follow-up. ADAC's case coordinators can act on the file the same day, and the documented sign-off chain supports any later medical or regulatory review without further requests back to Hurghada.
