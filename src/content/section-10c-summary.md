Alongside the Detailed Medical Report, HMC sends a short operational summary written for the ADAC case coordinator rather than for clinical archiving.

- Two- to three-line clinical headline
- Package code applied and disposition (discharged / observation / escalated)
- Any follow-up steps and target dates
- Open items requiring ADAC input
- Estimated case-closure date
