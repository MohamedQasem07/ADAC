A flat-rate package converts a multi-line negotiation into one number that everyone agrees on up front. Approval becomes faster because the cost is known. Billing becomes mechanical because the scope is defined.

- One number per clinical presentation — no surprises after treatment
- Pre-authorization is a yes/no decision against a published catalogue
- The physician chooses the package; the assistance team confirms scope
- Invoice line items map 1:1 onto the catalogue code (HMC-GI-02, HMC-OR-05, etc.)
- Case closure measured in hours, not weeks
