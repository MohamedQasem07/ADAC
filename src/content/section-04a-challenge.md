Travel medical cases concentrate the hardest parts of healthcare into a few hours: the traveler is unfamiliar with the local system, often in a hotel rather than a clinic, and the assistance company is reading clinical notes from a thousand kilometres away.

- Most ADAC cases present as GI, respiratory, or trauma — common in absolute terms, varied in severity
- Senior-skewed portfolio (62% over 60) raises medical complexity per case
- Hotel-room and remote-zone delivery is operationally different from a fixed clinic
- Language and documentation expectations vary by insurer
- Same-day reporting is non-negotiable for ADAC AG Holders
