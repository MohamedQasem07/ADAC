## Context

Hurghada Medical Center was built deliberately around the Red Sea tourist corridor — clinics placed where international case volume actually is, not where the real estate was cheap. Over five continuous years of operation we have grown into the region's largest private outpatient and emergency network for travelers, with an Operations Department that handles assistance and insurance casework as a primary discipline, not as an afterthought.

## Key points

- **Built around the corridor.** Ten clinical locations from Safaga in the north through Hurghada, Sahl Hasheesh and Soma Bay down to Marsa Alam — covering the resort zones where German travelers actually stay.
- **Continuous 24/7 operations.** Every location is staffed around the clock, with case coordinators reachable at any hour for assistance partners.
- **Dedicated assistance workflow.** Our Operations Department is structured for international assistance, with experience handling German insurance reporting, escalation and documentation since 2023.
- **German-language capable.** German-speaking physicians are available for hotel-room visits where possible, and English / Arabic / Russian cover the rest.
- **Outpatient and emergency focus.** The clinical core is travel medicine, urgent care, wound management and same-day case closure — exactly the case mix that ADAC AG Holders generate.

## What this means for ADAC

ADAC is not joining an unproven cooperation. The package framework being proposed sits on top of five years of real, multi-year case handling — built around traveler needs from day one.
