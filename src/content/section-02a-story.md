Hurghada Medical Center has operated continuously across the Red Sea coast for more than five years, focused on outpatient care and emergency response for international travelers. The network was built deliberately around the tourist corridor — clinics placed where the case volume is, not where the real estate was cheap.

- Largest private outpatient network in the Red Sea region
- 5+ years of structured experience with German assistance and insurance cases
- Operations Department with case-coordinator coverage 24/7
- German-speaking physicians available on hotel-room visits where possible
- Direct partnership history with ADAC AG Holder cases since 2023
