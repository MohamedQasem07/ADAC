## Context

Hurghada Medical Center was built deliberately around the Red Sea tourist corridor — clinics placed where international case volume actually is, not where the real estate was cheap. Over around a decade of continuous operation we have grown into the region's largest private outpatient and emergency network for travelers, with an Operations Department that handles assistance and insurance casework as a primary discipline, not as an afterthought.

## Key points

- **Built around the corridor.** Ten clinical locations from Safaga in the north through Hurghada, Sahl Hasheesh and Soma Bay down to Marsa Alam — covering the resort zones where German travelers actually stay.
- **Continuous 24/7 operations.** Every location is staffed around the clock, with case coordinators reachable at any hour for assistance partners.
- **Dedicated assistance workflow.** Our Operations Department is structured for international assistance, with experience handling German insurance reporting, escalation and documentation since 2023.
- **Multilingual coverage.** German-speaking physicians are available for hotel-room visits where possible, with Dutch, English, Arabic and Russian also covered across the team.
- **Outpatient and emergency focus.** The clinical core is travel medicine, urgent care, wound management and same-day case closure — exactly the case mix that ADAC AG Holders generate.

ADAC is not joining an unproven cooperation. The package framework being proposed sits on top of around a decade of real cooperation with ADAC travelers — built around traveler needs from day one. The data dashboard that follows shows the latest 2023–2026 analysed operational dataset.
