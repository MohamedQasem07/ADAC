Traditional itemized billing exposes both insurer and patient to unpredictable costs and slow approvals. Flat-rate packages collapse the decision into a single number that everyone agrees on up front.

For ADAC, this means fewer disputes, faster claims processing, and clearer expectations for travelers — while preserving the medical autonomy of the treating physician.
