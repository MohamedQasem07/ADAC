## Context

Claim cycle time in tourist-medical cases is usually limited by the back-and-forth between line-item invoices and free-form medical narratives. The HMC package framework eliminates both bottlenecks by standardizing the invoice format and pairing every invoice with a same-day structured medical report.

## Key points

- **One line per package.** The invoice carries package code, flat rate, and currency — no consumable-by-consumable adjudication.
- **Same-day medical report.** A Detailed Medical Report with ICD-10 coding is issued alongside the invoice, not weeks later.
- **Mappable to ADAC taxonomy.** Package codes map directly to ADAC's claims categories, so manual recoding drops out of the workflow.
- **83% closed within 48 hours.** Across the documented baseline, the majority of cases reach administrative closure inside two operational days.
- **Queries collapse to scope, not lines.** When a query is raised, it concerns scope-fit rather than line-item interpretation, which resolves faster.

## What this means for ADAC

The claims function targets a case-to-close cycle measured in hours rather than weeks. Reserves can be released quickly, the AG Holder file closes promptly, and the operational burden on ADAC's reconciliation team drops materially across the season.
