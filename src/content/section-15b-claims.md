---

The standard line of every invoice is the same: package code, flat rate, currency. The claims team reconciles against a published catalogue rather than against a free-form bill.

- One line item per package — no consumable-by-consumable adjudication
- Catalogue codes mappable directly to ADAC's claims taxonomy
- Same-day invoicing combined with same-day medical report
- Reconciliation becomes mechanical; queries drop sharply
- Case-to-close cycle time targeted at hours rather than weeks
