Sahl Hasheesh's resort cluster is far enough from central Hurghada that a return trip is operationally significant for an unwell traveler. Three on-zone locations remove that friction.

- Sahl Hasheesh Medical Center — main outpatient and ER
- Kay Soul Romance Clinic — hotel-zone access and visits
- Sahl Hasheesh Medical Center 2 — pediatric and observation cases
- Resort-gate access agreements in place with the major hotel groups
- Mobile clinic deployable for cases requiring extended on-site observation
