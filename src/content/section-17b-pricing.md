## Question

Which pricing direction becomes the negotiation baseline today, and how do we handle the small share of atypical cases?

## HMC suggested position

- **Flat-rate per package.** One single number per clinical presentation, applied consistently across all in-scope cases.
- **Standard tier as the opener.** Per-case review is reserved only for atypical mix that falls outside the agreed scope.
- **Negotiated paths kept available.** Alternative directions and "to be agreed" remain on the table for cases that genuinely need bespoke handling.
- **Presenter-facing setting only.** Pricing direction is operational language between Operations and ADAC claims — never displayed as a label to the audience.

## What we need ADAC to confirm

Agreement on the pricing direction for the pilot, signed off operationally between HMC Operations and ADAC claims before the June preparation phase closes.

## Risk / note

The pricing direction can be re-opened at the October review; mid-pilot changes require joint sign-off from both sides.
