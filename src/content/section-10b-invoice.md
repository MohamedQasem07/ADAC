The invoice is intentionally simple: one line per package, mapped 1:1 onto the catalogue. Reconciliation becomes mechanical.

- One line per package; price equals catalogue flat rate under the active partnership scenario
- Each line carries the catalogue code (HMC-GI-02, HMC-OR-05, …)
- Patient initials only; full identity in the medical report
- Currency: EUR; billing channel: direct to ADAC via Guarantee of Payment
- Same-day dispatch alongside the Detailed Medical Report
