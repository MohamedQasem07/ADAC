---

Communication during an escalation is structured and time-bound, because uncertainty during a clinical event is operationally expensive for everyone.

- ADAC notified within 15 minutes of escalation decision
- Preliminary clinical summary in the first notification (presenting complaint, red flag, action taken)
- Case coordinator stays on the line until clinical handoff to the receiving ER is complete
- Hourly status updates to ADAC during inpatient transition
- Full written incident report within 24 hours of case closure
