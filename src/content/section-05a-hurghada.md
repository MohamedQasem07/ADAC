Hurghada concentrates the bulk of HMC's outpatient volume and the bulk of German travelers staying on the coast. Six locations distribute response across the central tourist corridor.

- Pharaoh Azure Hotel branch — north corridor
- Minamark Hotel branch — north corridor
- Sheraton Hotel branch — central
- Al-Kawther branch — labs and X-Ray support
- Tourist Promenade Clinic 1 — main outpatient and ER capability
- Tourist Promenade Clinic 2 — pediatric and high-volume hours
- 24/7 cover across all six; mobile clinic unit deployable to hotel gates
