The traveler stays in the hotel room. A physician plus nurse arrive with full portable equipment and complete the same workup that a clinic visit would.

- Same package price as a clinic visit — mode of delivery does not change pricing
- Portable IV station, ECG, SpO₂, nebulizer, wound-care field kit, ear-wash
- Portable X-Ray available for trauma triage at the hotel
- German-speaking physician available on request
- Recommended for: GI cases with mild-to-moderate dehydration, respiratory cases, wound care, sprains
