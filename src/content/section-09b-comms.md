Communication runs through a small number of well-defined channels so that nothing is lost.

- Named case coordinator per case for the full lifecycle
- Email for case notification, reports, and invoicing
- Phone hotline for urgent escalation and live clinical handoffs
- Optional secure portal for case dashboards and historical lookups
- All clinical communications in English by default; German on request
