- Same Detailed Medical Report format for every case
- Same flat-rate billing — no surprise invoices
- Independent public reviews on Google, TripAdvisor, and Trustpilot
- Long-standing reputation in the Hurghada tourist-medical sector
- AG-Holder feedback collected anonymously and reported back to ADAC monthly
