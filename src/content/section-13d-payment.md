Pricing and payment are intentionally simple. The full catalogue is in one currency, billed directly to ADAC.

- Currency: **EUR** across all packages and scenarios
- Direct billing to ADAC via **Guarantee of Payment (GOP)**
- **Pre-authorization** required for packages above **EUR 250** and for all admission decisions
- Acknowledgement of case notification within 15 minutes; full triage decision within 30 minutes
- Payment terms agreed in the bilateral partnership contract
- Bank details and reconciliation channel handled in the contract, not on the projected deck
