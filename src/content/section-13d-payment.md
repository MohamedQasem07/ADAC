## Context

The financial layer is intentionally simple. The catalogue is denominated in a single currency, settlement runs through a Guarantee of Payment arrangement where applicable, and a clear pre-authorization threshold separates routine cases from cases that require ADAC sign-off before work proceeds. Bank-account details are handled in separate financial paperwork outside the meeting room.

## Key points

- **Currency: EUR.** All 65 packages are denominated in euros across both partnership tiers.
- **GOP direct billing.** Settlement runs through Guarantee of Payment direct billing to ADAC where applicable, rather than out-of-pocket reimbursement by the traveler.
- **EUR 250 pre-authorization threshold.** Packages priced above EUR 250, and all admission decisions, require pre-authorization before work begins.
- **Acknowledgement and triage timing.** Case notification is acknowledged within 15 minutes; the full triage decision is returned within 30 minutes.
- **Bank details handled separately.** Account information is exchanged via the bilateral financial paperwork, not on projected slides.

## What this means for ADAC

ADAC settles in a single currency through an existing direct-billing mechanism, with a clear cost threshold above which authorization is required. The deliberate exclusion of bank-account details from projected material reflects a documentation standard that ADAC's compliance and finance teams should recognize as appropriate for an executive review.
