## Question

Do we confirm the single package-coded invoice line per case, with an optional supporting breakdown attached on request?

## HMC suggested position

- **One package-coded line per case.** Each invoice carries a single line referencing the case ID and date of service, mapped to the agreed catalogue.
- **Supporting breakdown on request.** Medication and consumable detail attached as a separate supporting document when ADAC claims needs the line-by-line view.
- **Multi-package single-case scenarios itemised.** Rare cases where one encounter triggers more than one package are billed with explicit itemisation, not bundled.
- **Escalation events on a separate line.** Ambulance, ER coordination, and any escalation-driven cost appears as a clearly marked separate line, never inside the package line.

## What we need ADAC to confirm

Confirm the single-line invoice format and the trigger conditions under which ADAC wants the supporting breakdown attached by default.

## Risk / note

Multi-package single-case scenarios are rare in the observed mix but possible, and are itemised in full when they occur.
