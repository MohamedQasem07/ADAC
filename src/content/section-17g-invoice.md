Confirm the operational format of the invoice and the mapping into ADAC's claims taxonomy.

- One line per package, with catalogue code (§10.2)
- Currency EUR; direct billing via Guarantee of Payment
- Invoice sent alongside the Detailed Medical Report on the same day
- Mapping of catalogue codes to ADAC claim categories (joint exercise during June prep)
- Reconciliation cadence: per-case vs monthly bulk
