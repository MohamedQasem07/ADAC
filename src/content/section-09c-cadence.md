## Context

Reporting runs at four cadences — per case, per week, per month, and per quarter. Each layer answers a different question for ADAC, from individual case closure through aggregate trend lines. The cadence is fixed in advance so ADAC always knows what arrives when, without having to chase.

## Key points

- **Same-day.** Detailed medical report and package-coded invoice issued on the operational day the case closes.
- **Weekly digest.** Active-case summary covering every open file — escalated, in observation, awaiting follow-up — sent on a fixed weekday.
- **Monthly performance summary.** Response-time hit-rates, package mix, severity mix, escalation rate, and same-day-closure rate against the 83% within 48h benchmark.
- **Quarterly business review.** On request, a deeper read on trends, operational notes, and any structural changes — held jointly with the HMC operations lead.
- **Ad-hoc incident reports.** Any escalated case generates an incident report within 24 hours of closure, attached to the same case file.

## What this means for ADAC

ADAC has a predictable reporting rhythm with no surprises. The same metrics are used at every cadence, so trend lines stay readable across months and quarters, and ADAC can audit individual cases or aggregate performance from the same data set.
