Reporting runs at three cadences — daily for individual cases, weekly for active cases, monthly for aggregate performance.

- **Same-day** — Detailed Medical Report and package-coded invoice for each closed case
- **Weekly** — active-case digest with status of every open case (escalated, in observation, awaiting follow-up)
- **Monthly** — performance summary with SLA hit-rates, package mix, escalation count, and any operational notes
- **Ad-hoc** — incident reports for any case requiring escalation, within 24 hours of closure
