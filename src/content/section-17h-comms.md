## Question

Which channel becomes the primary for the pilot — email, portal, or phone — and who covers the after-hours escalation line?

## HMC suggested position

- **Email as the operational primary.** Case notification, status updates, and report dispatch all move through email as the documented channel of record.
- **Phone for time-critical exchanges.** Reserved for red flags, missing GOP, and active escalation where written latency is unacceptable.
- **Assistance portal for archival.** Used for structured handover and long-term archival of case documentation alongside the live email thread.
- **Named case coordinator at HMC Operations.** Every case gets an assigned coordinator at HMC, with a documented counterpart expected on the ADAC side.

## What we need ADAC to confirm

Confirm the primary channel and the after-hours escalation contact point on the ADAC side, including weekend and public-holiday coverage during the pilot.

## Risk / note

After-hours phone routes to the on-call coordinator on the HMC side; ADAC needs the same coverage on its side for the pilot to function symmetrically.
