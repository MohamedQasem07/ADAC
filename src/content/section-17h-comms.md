How the two teams communicate during routine cases and during escalations.

- Primary channel for case notification (email, portal, or hotline)
- Hotline for urgent escalation — confirmed number on each side
- Named case coordinator per case at HMC; named counterpart at ADAC
- Working language: English by default, German on request
- After-hours coverage — 24/7 on the HMC side; ADAC weekend/holiday coverage to confirm
