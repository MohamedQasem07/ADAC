A small set of items is intentionally outside the standard package scope. These are always quoted before any work begins, never added silently to the invoice.

- Cases meeting escalation criteria (transferred to ER pathway — billed separately)
- Inpatient admission of any kind
- Multi-day follow-up beyond the package-included visit
- Specialty consults outside the standard outpatient scope (cardiology workup, etc.)
- Wounds with deep tissue involvement, suspected nerve/tendon damage, or older than 12 hours
- Procedures not listed in the catalogue — quoted on request, requires pre-authorization
