Mode of service is chosen by combining severity with the AG Holder's mobility, location, and the equipment needed.

- **Clinic visit** preferred for trauma-with-imaging and multi-hour observation cases
- **Hotel-room visit** preferred when the AG Holder is unwell, the case is green or yellow, and portable equipment is sufficient
- **Mobile clinic unit** chosen for extended (3+ hr) cases or multi-AG-Holder situations at a resort
- Distance and traffic factored into dispatch — nearest equipped location wins
- All three modes carry the same flat-rate package price
