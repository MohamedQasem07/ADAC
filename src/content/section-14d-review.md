The October joint review converts pilot data into a scale decision. The framework either continues as the default for ADAC Red Sea cases, gets adjusted, or is wound down — based on the data, not on impressions.

- Joint review meeting based on the three monthly performance reports
- SLA hit-rates reviewed against agreed targets
- Financial outcomes reviewed (case cost predictability, dispute count)
- AG-Holder feedback summary (where collected)
- Decision: continue, adjust, or wind down
- If continuing: terms extended; if adjusting: revised package set agreed for 2027
