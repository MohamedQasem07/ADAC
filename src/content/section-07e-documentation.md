Documentation starts at the bedside and finalizes the same day. The medical report follows the structure shown in §10.1 and is dispatched to ADAC and the AG Holder.

- Real-time clinical notes captured during treatment
- Structured Detailed Medical Report drafted by the treating physician
- Same-day dispatch to ADAC by the agreed channel
- Report includes package code, treatment summary, disposition, and safety-net advice
- Available in English; German on request
