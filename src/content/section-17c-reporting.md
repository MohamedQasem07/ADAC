---

Confirm the structure of the three report cadences (§9.3) so that the pilot starts with everything in place.

- Same-day Detailed Medical Report — template approved as shown in §10.1
- Same-day case operational summary — short format for the ADAC case coordinator
- Weekly active-case digest — fields and recipient list
- Monthly performance report — KPIs to include, format (PDF, dashboard, or both)
- Ad-hoc incident report format for escalations
