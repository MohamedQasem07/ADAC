Language is treated as a clinical-quality issue, not a convenience. Travelers describe symptoms more precisely in their own language; physicians document more precisely when they understand.

- Clinical conversations in English by default
- German-speaking physician available on request for hotel-room visits
- Operations Department covers English, German, Arabic, Russian
- Medical reports available in English (German on request)
- Written safety-net advice given in the AG Holder's preferred language where staffing allows
