## Context

Notification is the moment the case file opens. ADAC contacts HMC Operations through one of the agreed channels — email, partner portal or phone hotline — and intake begins. The window between notification and acknowledgement is the first measurable point in the journey, and it sets the tempo for everything that follows. Triage starts in parallel with administrative setup; nothing waits for paperwork.

## Key points

- **Three notification channels.** Email, partner portal and 24/7 phone hotline — ADAC chooses the channel that fits the urgency.
- **Acknowledgement target.** HMC Operations confirms receipt of the notification within the agreed SLA window, with a case reference returned to ADAC.
- **Named case coordinator.** A single coordinator is assigned for the duration of the case, so ADAC always has one point of contact.
- **Minimum viable intake.** Location, presenting complaint, age and traveler ID are captured first; the rest is filled in during triage.
- **Parallel triage start.** Clinical triage begins immediately on the information at hand, not after full administrative intake.

## What this means for ADAC

A predictable, time-bounded entry point into the HMC workflow. ADAC knows the case is open, who owns it on the HMC side and what to expect next. That clarity at the front of the journey is what makes the rest of the SLA chain — triage, assignment, treatment, invoice — measurable.
