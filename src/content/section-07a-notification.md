The journey starts the moment ADAC opens the case. The Operations Department receives the notification through the agreed channel and assigns a case coordinator.

- ADAC opens the case via email, portal, or phone hotline
- HMC Operations Department acknowledges receipt within the agreed target
- A named case coordinator is assigned for the duration of the case
- Initial AG Holder details (location, presenting complaint, age) recorded
- Triage starts immediately — no waiting for full paperwork
