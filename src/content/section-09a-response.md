Response times are operational targets, not legally binding guarantees. They are monitored continuously and reported monthly to ADAC.

- Acknowledgement of case notification: **target ≤ 15 minutes**
- Full triage decision and package proposal: **target ≤ 30 minutes**
- Physician dispatch for hotel-room visits: **target ≤ 45 minutes** in central zones
- Same-day Detailed Medical Report: **target same operational day**
- Escalation notification to ADAC for red-flag cases: **target ≤ 15 minutes** from decision
