## Context

Clinic Visit is the standard delivery mode when the traveler is mobile and clinically suitable for transport. The case runs through a fixed sequence — reception, triage, physician examination, treatment, documentation, discharge — inside a controlled clinical environment with full diagnostic backing. This is the default mode for most ADAC cases and the baseline against which the other two modes are measured.

## Key points

- **Coverage footprint.** 10 HMC clinic locations across Hurghada, Sahl Hasheesh, Safaga and Marsa Alam, all on 24/7 cover.
- **On-site diagnostics.** Larger locations carry X-Ray, laboratory and observation capacity, which removes most reasons for external referral.
- **Fixed workflow.** Reception, triage, exam, treatment, documentation and discharge are sequenced the same way for every traveler, every time.
- **Same package price as the other two modes.** Pricing is set by the assigned package code, not by where the care is delivered.
- **Best clinical fit.** Cases needing imaging, multi-hour observation or layered diagnostics are routed here by triage as a matter of protocol.

## What this means for ADAC

Clinic Visit gives ADAC the most predictable case profile — a controlled environment, full diagnostic stack and a documented workflow. The result is faster, cleaner case closure and a medical report that holds up under reimbursement review, with no pricing variance introduced by the choice of location.
