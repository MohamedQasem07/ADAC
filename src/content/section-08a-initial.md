Triage begins on first contact, whether by phone or on physician arrival. A short structured checklist captures the essentials needed to choose the right package and mode.

- Presenting complaint, duration, severity
- Vital signs and obvious red flags (chest pain, breathing, hemodynamic instability)
- Age, key medical history, current medications, allergies
- AG Holder location and traveler mobility
- Language preference and accompanying companion
